About Me

Since I was 10, I've been fascinated with the world of cybersecurity, starting with how phishing attacks work. That childhood curiosity quickly turned into a passion and a goal, and at 20, I achieved it by becoming a Penetration Tester. Today, I mostly focus on web and mobile applications, as well as source code review, always learning new methods and keeping up with the latest trends in the hacking world.

My work doesn't stop with professional engagements. As the founder of The Free Security, I've dedicated myself to the mission of giving everyone the chance to get the same knowledge and resources I had on my journey. I believe that cybersecurity is something everyone should understand, and through the organization, I want to make a positive impact on my community and country, as much as I can.

In my free time, I love solving riddles and mysteries and always try to understand how things work "under the hood." That curiosity guides me in both my professional and personal life, constantly inspiring me to explore and find new solutions.
