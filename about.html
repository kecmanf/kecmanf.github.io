About Me

Hello! My name is Filip Kecman. I am a dedicated penetration tester with a passion for uncovering vulnerabilities and strengthening digital security. My work is driven by the belief that a secure internet is a fundamental right for everyone.

Beyond my professional work, I am the proud founder of a free security non-profit organization. We are committed to providing cybersecurity education and resources to communities that lack access to them. Our mission is to empower individuals and small businesses with the knowledge they need to protect themselves from cyber threats.

In my free time, I enjoy writing about the latest trends in cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and the importance of digital literacy. This portfolio serves as a hub for my work, thoughts, and community initiatives. I'm always open to connecting with fellow professionals and enthusiasts, so please feel free to reach out.
